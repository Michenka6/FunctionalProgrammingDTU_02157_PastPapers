40.Hunter Squad Assistant -3
Ting.

[Agility stat has increased by 1.]

“I got a boost from this?”

It was surprising. If it was any other Hunter, their stats would have increased consistently, but he was different. He had already received extensive training with high stats, so his growth in stats was bound to be slower.

“Only one point since training camp until now.”

He didn’t expect much, but at least he got something. To put it in gaming terms, it was like a level 100 adventurer hunting thousands of level 1 snails.

“Better than nothing.”

Special obstacle course training went on all morning. The trainees were about to face the final challenge, the tightrope walking course.

“I’m pleased with the trainees’ attitude towards training today. Let’s continue to work hard for the remaining training! Do you understand?”

“Yes, sir!”

They were currently lined up in a training area with minimal safety precautions.

“Whoa… it’s so dizzying.”

“That rappelling training earlier was just a joke….”

The hunters swallowed their saliva as they looked down the cliff.

In the last expedition, they formally went through tightrope walking training, but this time it was completely different.

“The length of the tightrope in front of you is 100m. The left rope is the 1st rope, and the right rope is the 2nd rope. Grab onto these ropes and cross one by one. Do you understand?”

“Yes, sir!”

“Trainee number 206! What’s the reason why we’re doing this training?”

Kim Min-joon asked one of the trainees during the explanation.

“Tightrope walking is mainly used when stuck in a swamp or poison pond during dungeon raids, where it is not possible to move by conventional means.”

Son Eun-seo answered flawlessly, as if it was nothing.

“I asked because he seemed distracted. What a waste.”

Kim Min-joon demonstrated by attaching a safety rope to the rope.

“This training is probably the most difficult for other hunters.”

When regular soldiers trained for tightrope walking, they would move about 50m in distance. But the Hunter squad had to rely on a single 100m rope. On top of that, the training captain releases flying-type monsters in the middle.

“Small Pekka. It looked like a rock lizard at first glance.”

Small Pekka was a low-level monster like the Hound, but in the situation of tightrope walking, it was no different from a demon.

Moreover, he was wearing an 80kg power suit…

 

“Kim Min-joon positioned himself in the horse stance after hooking his right ankle on the tightrope. He maintained a perfect posture for a certain amount of time so that the trainees could observe and follow him.

“Horse stance!”

Upon hearing the other instructor’s command, Kim Min-joon began crossing the tightrope.

‘Going at this speed should be enough.’

Normally, he would have crossed quickly, but he was currently an instructor. He needed to consider the trainees and purposely slow down his pace.

“Starting now, monsters will be released!”

After passing the 50m mark, the head of education on the other side released two small peckers.

“Kikikikik!”

“Kikik!”

Then small birds with long beaks attacked Kim Min-joon relentlessly.

“Whip! Whiz! Whip!”

The small peckers aimed for his head, arms, and legs.

‘Oh, damn it. I almost killed them.’

Remembering the head of education’s instruction not to kill the monsters, Kim Min-joon focused on defense, moving his arms and legs to fend off the attacks.

“Kikik!”

“Kikikikik!”

As their attacks kept missing, the small peckers became more frantic and swung their beaks with all their might.

‘Don’t act cocky.’

Kim Min-joon aimed and threw a dart at one of the small peckers.

“Squeak…”

The small pecker that was hit by the dart retreated in fear.

“Kiki, kikik!”

The other small pecker also flapped its wings and flew away.

“What, are these guys?”

 

“The education sergeant quickly grabbed the rope tied around the neck of the Small Packer and retrieved him.”

“Wow!”

After Kim Min-joon finished his demonstration, the hunters looked bewildered.

“What was that? Did he just throw a punch?”

“He’s a real stat monster. This was supposed to be a training that had to hit, but he didn’t get hit once.”

“Is that just what it is? Small Packers might be scared of that movement?”

“Oh, really?”

After that, the hunters crossed the line and tried to throw a punch at the Small Packer, but they were met with the angry bird’s beak attack.

“Eek! Don’t come near me!”

“Hmph. Even the daughter of the captain has no choice but to face that?”

Kim Min-joon raised the corners of his mouth as he watched Son Eun-seo struggle.

“For some reason, it’s funny to watch our squad members and that guy get beaten up.”

The second day of training passed like that, and the third day arrived.

“Finally! It’s the day of the Chamho combat training!”

“Is the training really that good?”

“Just do it all by yourself.”

The squad members looked at Kim Min-joon, who was humming a tune, as if they were tired of it.

“I’ll give you a high-level piece of information. The squad that wins the Chamho combat training gets one squad out-of-bounds pass from the education sergeant.”

“Oh?”

“Really?”

At the mention of a squad out-of-bounds pass, the eyes of the squad members lit up.

Nearby hunters were the same.

“Kim Min-joon. Are you going to participate in the Chamho combat training too?”

“Sergeant Kim Min-joon. I am also participating.”

“Oh, really? Be careful if you meet me. I won first place in the last Chamho combat training.”

Choi Seung-woo, who had suffered through an incredible hell during the PT exercise before, patted him on the shoulder and passed by.

“Oh? Look at this guy?”

It seemed like he had accumulated emotions due to the PT exercise.

“Alright! The third day of the combat training has arrived! The education sergeant will observe the trainees’ attitudes today and decide whether or not to shorten the recreational time on the last day!”

“Ah!”

“Ah!”

In front of the Chamho combat training ground.

The education sergeant uttered the unexpected words of recreational time.

“Wow… really?”

“I didn’t expect anything because the training intensity was insane.”

 

In that statement, the students’ eyes lit up with enthusiasm.

On the fourth day of typical field training, in the evening, as a way of rewarding the trainees for their hard work, they were given recreational time, such as courage training.

They didn’t expect such an activity during this field training, but there was a possibility of it happening.

“Even if someone faints in the middle of it, we’ll go until the end!”

This recreational time was an excellent opportunity for male and female trainees to strengthen their friendships.

They decided to take the “Chamho” combat seriously.

“Alright! The commanding officer will grant an outside pass to the winning team. Conversely, the platoon leader will conduct separate PT exercises for the underperforming team.”

The commanding officer said that even though it was a “Chamho” combat, they should approach it as if it were a real battle.

“Wow, muddy water. This is the Chamho combat!”

There was even a large pit that could accommodate one squad.

Kim Min-jun waited eagerly for his turn.

“All Chamho combats end in one round. It will be conducted by platoon, regardless of gender and rank.”

“Ha!”

As the instructors completed the platoon assignments, the hunters’ eyes scanned Kim Min-jun’s squad.

“Look at the pressure coming in already.”

“It’s because of Min-jun. His strength and agility stats are at a monster level!”

“Rather than that, let’s form the line. Let’s have Seung-ho and Min-jun at the front.”

It was not a leave pass, but the hunters prepared for the Chamho combat with enthusiasm.

“Let’s begin with the 2nd squad and 2nd platoon and the 3rd squad and 3rd platoon!”

“Ha!”

Their squad was the first to be up for the match.

The opposing squad included Sergeant Choi Seung-woo.

It was the same squad that won in the previous Chamho combat.

“Kim Min-jun. Show us a magic trick where your strength stat of 40 beats 60.”

“I’ll do my best.”

“No way, insane! But why does the Lieutenant happen to be on that side?”

The squad members were dismayed to see that there was one officer on the 3rd platoon.

“It’s all about luck and skill. You guys know that too, right?”

Choi Seung-woo lowered his body and raised the corners of his mouth.

“Uh…should we switch sides?”

The members of Kim Min-jun’s squad became anxious and quickly discussed their opinions.

“Let’s keep the sides the same. Min-jun and I will take care of Lieutenant and Choi Seung-woo over there. You guys, keep them from getting too close to us until then.”

“Got it!”

Under the direction of Sergeant Lee Seung-ho, the squad members took their positions.

“……”

For about 10 seconds, there was silence between the squad members.

Beep!

“Let’s gooooo!”

“Yaaaaaaa!”

When the commanding officer’s whistle sounded, the Chamho combat began.

 

Sure, shall we test out the skill now?

I’ve been itching to use this skill since earlier.

“I’ve already activated the singularity of my magic.”

With that, Kim Min-joon’s magic takes on the form of a large hand and pulls Choi Seung-woo towards him.

“Uh, what’s going on?”

Choi Seung-woo, caught off guard by the sudden force, tumbles forward.

“Oof, Sergeant Choi Seung-woo. You should lose some weight.”

Kim Min-joon casually grabs his waist and lifts him up above his head.

“What the hell was that just now? Are you insane?”

“Sergeant Choi Seung-woo, you were rolling around so brilliantly. Did you trip on something? I thought you were even seeing Rampage.”

Whoosh!

“Ugh!”

That’s how overconfident Choi Seung-woo gets thrown out of the tent in just 10 seconds.

“Oh, those two look alike.”

When Kim Min-joon turns his gaze, he sees Sergeant Lee Seung-ho wrestling with another sergeant, their hands locked together.

“Kyaah!”

“Kraaak!”

Sergeant Lee Seung-ho is slightly losing the match.

“Let’s leave them alone for now. They’re fighting one-on-one, so it’s impolite to interfere.”

But first, we need to take care of our own platoon members!

“Black magic grab!”

Kim Min-joon uses his magic hand to pull the platoon members who are about to be thrown out of the tent towards him.

Then, the platoon members roll towards him as if possessed.

“What was that just now?”

“Oof, I have no idea. Something suddenly pulled us.”

“But more importantly, Sergeant Lee Seung-ho got thrown out!”

One of the platoon members points towards where Lee Seung-ho is.

“Give up, you guys!”

There stands a sergeant who is stretching his neck and shoulders.
